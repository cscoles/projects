  • Features Overview

  • What is the PSR Registration Application for?

    This PSR Registration Application will allow families to create accounts and register their children for PSR classes through the Parish. This web application will streamline the process and allow parents to register their children in less time than was previously required.

  • How will this new system save me time?

    The new PSR registration system will allow parents to fill out the information for their family and their students once, instead of having to fill otu the same information for ecach year. Additionally the new system will allow the PSR administrators to track your students as they progress through the sacrements.

  • Family Portal

  • What is the family portal?

    The family portal is how you will access this new registration system. Upon logging in, parents will have access to their family information and students information. This is where parents will be able to update their contact information, register new students for PSR classes, and select PSR sessions for their students to attend.

  • Administration Portal

  • What is the administration portal?

    The administration portal is the portal through which the PSR administrators will have access. This portal will provide them with all of the reports, conact information, and family and student information needed to efficiently and effectively run the PSR program. The PSR administrators will be the only ones with access to this portal.

  • Technical Support

  • What technical support is provided?

    For the famalies using the PSR registration app, please consult the Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) section of this help page for assistance. If you are unable to find an answer to your issue in the FAQ, please use the available contact information for the PSR office, or contact the St. Joseph Parish Office for further assistance.

  • Security Concerns

  • Who has access to my information?

    Any information that you provide through the registration portal will be visable only to yourself. The only way to access your information will be with the username and password that you create when you register for an account. For this reason it is imperative that you protect your login information, and if you think someone may have acquired your login info, it is strongly suggested that you update your password. Outside of your own account, the only other access to your information will be the access granted to the PSR Administration, who will have access to the information as needed to run the program.

  • How is my information protected?

    Your information is important, as is its security. To protect your information the registration app requires a valid username and password to login. Additionally, all data entered and stored through the app is kept on secure data servers that are constantly updated, and meet all security regulations to ensure your data is kept safe.

  • FAQ

  • How do I register for an account?

    To create an account, select the Create New Account link under the login boxes. This will take you to the appropriate page where you will create your username and password for your new account. Once you have registered for an account, you will immediately be able to login using your newly created username and password.

  • How do I login?

    To login, enter your username and password in the login boxes and select enter. You will then be redirected to your account page.

  • What if I forgot my password?

    If you forget your password, select the Forgot My Password link under the account login boxes. Selecting this link will take you to a page where you will be able to change your password so that you may login to your account.

  • How do I add my families' information?

    To add your families’’ information, first login to the family portal. Once you have logged in to the family portal, you can access the family tab. Under the family tab you will be presented with a form asking you for the information required by the PSR administration. Fill out this form completely, and then select the Add New Family button. This will create your family and send the information to the PSR administration team.

  • How do I add my children’s information?

    Once you have created your family, you can then add your student or students. To add a new student, click on the student tab at the top of the portal. This will present you with the form for all required information required by the PSR administration. Once you complete the form, select the “Add New Student” button. This will add your new student, and send the information on to the PSR administration team.

  • Where can I find the User's Manual?

    The Registration User's Manual contains more detailed information related to the family and administration portal for the registration application. It is complete with screenshots, notes, and helpful hints that will guide the user through the various features of the application.
